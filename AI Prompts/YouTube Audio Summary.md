When you generate an Audio Overview, you can now provide a **Custom Prompt** to guide the hosts. This prevents them from being too "cheery" and forces them to be useful.

**For Triage (The "Is this worth my time?" Prompt)**

Focus this deep dive on technical substance. I am trying to decide if I need to watch these full videos. Act as a critical filter: identify the 3 most unique insights from each video that aren't common knowledge. Point out if a video is mostly fluff or if it contains specific 'how-to' steps I should go back and see visually.

**For Knowledge Extraction (The "Study Buddy" Prompt)**

Explain these videos like a masterclass. Connect the ideas between Video A and Video B. Use analogies for the complex parts, but keep the terminology accurate. End the segment for each video by telling me exactly at what timestamp the most important visual demonstration occurs.
