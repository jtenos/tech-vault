### The one-line guardrail against over-engineering

At `high` and `xhigh`, Opus 4.8 will occasionally produce a multi-step plan for a change that needed one line.

**This kills it:**

```
If the fix is a one-line change, just make it. Don't write a plan.
```

**What it does:** short-circuits the over-thinking failure on trivial sub-tasks without lowering effort for the whole session. I underestimated this one for weeks; it removes more friction than it has any right to.
